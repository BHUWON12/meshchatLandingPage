Hop-to-Hop Connections

MeshChat leverages hop-to-hop (multi-hop mesh) networking, where messages are relayed from device to device until they reach their destination—even if there’s no direct connection. This dynamic routing ensures your messages get delivered reliably, even in challenging environments, by finding the best available path through nearby peers.
